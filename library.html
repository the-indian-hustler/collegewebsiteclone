Library (भारतीय सूचना प्रौद्योगिकी संस्थान कोटा)
OPAC Library IIIT Kota IIIT Kota Central Library was inaugurated on October 3, 2019.
The Central Library is located at Prabha Bhavan, 2nd floor, MNIT Jaipur campus.
For smooth functioning of the library and coordination with all faculty members,
students and staff, Library Committee and Library Advisory Committee for IIIT Kota have been constituted.
IIIT Kota Central Library provides book bank facility to IIIT Kota
undergraduate students which was started in July 2018.
Per day circulation of books and number of visitors in library is around 41 and 113 respectively.
Total numbers of books in IIIT Kota library are 5948 (as on 31.03.2020).
Additionally, 7 vendors were empanelled in the session 2019-20 and the books
were purchased through Vendors in this year.
